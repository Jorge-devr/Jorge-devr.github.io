Fernet & Coke

Description

It is the most popular drink in Argentina, which mixes the bitter taste of the originally medicinal drink from Italy, Fernet, mixed with the very sweet and effervescent taste of Coca-Cola.

Ingredients

  • 50 milliliters of Fernet Branca
  • 200 milliliters of Coca-cola
  • 3 ice cubes

Preparation

Step 1:

In a long drink glass, whose recommended proportion is: 30% Fernet, 70% coca and three ice cubes.

Step 2:

Put the ice, pour the Fernet, and then add the Coca-Cola little by little (everything is going to overturn due to the gas), and keep tasting until it has the desired flavor, neither too sweet nor too bitter.
